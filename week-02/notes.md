# Week 2: Networking & Protocol Analysis
This week focused on Layer 3 and Layer 4 of the OSI model, specifically subnetting, routing, and protocol interrogation. During the Invisible Architecture module, I utilized `ip route add` to manually reconstruct a corrupted routing table and restore external gateway connectivity. In the Protocol Interrogation session, I deployed `ss -tuln` (Socket Statistics) to uncover cloaked services listening on hidden ports, bypassing the need for aggressive external scanning. This knowledge was directly applied in **TLAB-02: Operation Blackout**, where I remediated a sabotaged network subnet, cleansed poisoned DNS entries in `/etc/hosts`, and captured a raw TCP 3-way handshake as forensic evidence.
