# Week 7: Reconnaissance & Vulnerability Analysis
The Intelligence Cycle was utilized to map external attack surfaces without direct interaction (OSINT) and via active enumeration. I deployed Sublist3r for DNS enumeration and Shodan for passive IoT profiling to bypass active IDS triggers. During active reconnaissance, I utilized `nmap -sV` to complete the TCP handshake and extract precise software version banners, allowing me to identify outdated, vulnerable Nginx services. This methodology culminated in **TLAB-07: Operation Shadow Map**, where I generated a professional Threat Profile prioritizing vulnerabilities based on real-world Likelihood and Impact rather than pure CVSS scoring.
