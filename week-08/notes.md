# Week 8: Exploitation Frameworks & Privilege Escalation
This week transitioned into offensive Red Team operations. I initialized `msfconsole` to leverage the Metasploit Framework, utilizing pre-built exploit modules and payloads to gain initial access to vulnerable targets. Following initial compromise, I explored Privilege Escalation techniques, specifically exploiting misconfigured SUID binaries. A critical command executed was `sudo awk 'BEGIN {system("/bin/sh")}'`, which leveraged GTFOBins to bypass restricted permissions and spawn a root-level shell. These tactics were deployed in **TLAB-08: Operation Deep Pivot**, successfully mapping a hidden network and establishing persistence.
